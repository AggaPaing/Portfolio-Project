Louis Vuitton Pacific Chill

Blackcurrent and cedrat combine in a invigorating accord, like day break over the ocean.

Evoking the regenerative vigor of the infinite ocean, Pacific Chill translates the pleasure of the warmth of a new day on the skin. An audacious olfactory creation by Master Perfumer Jacques Canvallier Belletrud, this detox-oriented cologne captues nature's vitality which revives the senses and opens the mind. The vibrant blackcurrant is enhanced by notes of cedrat and lemon, a combination of coriander seeds and ambrette, basil, peppermint and orange, creting an invigorating impact. This serene creation with soft fruity notees smoothes the soul, like a desire for the horizontal. With its light and airy composition, LV Pacific Chill effortlessly mirrors the vibrant energy of the season, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a fragrance that encapsulates the spirit of springtime with sophistication and style.
